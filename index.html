Canada Take-Home Pay Calculator

This Canada take-home pay calculator helps you estimate your after-tax income based on your annual salary, province or territory, and pay frequency. It is designed for Canadians who want a fast and simple way to understand how much income they actually take home.

You can include RRSP and FHSA contributions to see how tax-advantaged savings may reduce your taxable income. Results update instantly, making this calculator useful for budgeting, financial planning, and comparing income scenarios.

How This Canadian Income Tax Calculator Works

Your taxable income is calculated by subtracting RRSP and FHSA contributions from your gross income. Simplified federal and provincial tax estimates are applied and results are displayed based on your selected pay frequency.

  • Estimated net (after-tax) income
  • Total taxes paid
  • Federal income tax
  • Provincial or territorial income tax

Is This Take-Home Pay Calculator Accurate?

This calculator provides estimates only and does not include every possible tax credit, deduction, CPP, EI, or province-specific rule. Actual taxes may differ based on your personal situation and CRA regulations.

For official tax calculations or filing, consult the Canada Revenue Agency or a qualified tax professional.
